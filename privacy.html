Privacy Policy

Qur’an Quotes – Daily Ayahs & Islamic Inspiration is a privacy-focused Chrome extension. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

Information Collection

This extension does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal, sensitive, or identifiable user data.

Local Storage

The extension uses Chrome’s local storage only to save:

  • Favorite Qur’an ayahs
  • Last viewed ayah
  • User interface and display preferences

All data remains on your device and is never sent to any external server.

Permissions

  • storage – to save favorites and preferences locally
  • notifications – to display daily Qur’an ayah reminders
  • alarms – to schedule daily ayah notifications

Third-Party Services

This extension does not use analytics, advertising, tracking technologies, or any third-party services.

Policy Updates

This privacy policy may be updated if the extension’s features or functionality change.
