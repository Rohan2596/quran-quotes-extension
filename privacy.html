Privacy Policy

Bible Quotes is a privacy-focused Chrome extension. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

Information Collection

This extension does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal or sensitive user data.

Local Storage

The extension uses Chrome’s local storage only to save:

  • Favorite Bible verses
  • Last viewed verse
  • User interface preferences

All data remains on your device and is never sent to external servers.

Permissions

  • storage – to save favorites and preferences locally
  • notifications – to display daily Bible verse notifications
  • alarms – to schedule daily notifications

Third-Party Services

This extension does not use analytics, advertising, tracking, or any third-party services.

Policy Updates

This privacy policy may be updated if the extension’s functionality changes.
