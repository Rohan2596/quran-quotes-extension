Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Qur’an Quotes – Daily Ayahs & Islamic Inspiration, a Chrome browser extension, handles user data and protects your privacy.

Overview

Qur’an Quotes is a faith-based Chrome extension designed to provide daily Qur’an ayahs, Islamic reminders, and inspirational quotes. Your privacy is extremely important to us.

Information Collection

This extension does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal, sensitive, or personally identifiable information.

Local Data Storage

Qur’an Quotes uses Chrome’s built-in local storage exclusively to improve your experience. The following data may be stored locally on your device:

  • Saved or favorite Qur’an ayahs
  • Last viewed ayah
  • User interface preferences (such as display settings)

This data never leaves your browser and is not accessible by us or any third party.

Permissions Explanation

The extension requests only the minimum permissions required for its functionality:

  • storage – Used to save your favorite ayahs and preferences locally
  • notifications – Used to display daily Qur’an ayah reminders
  • alarms – Used to schedule daily Islamic reminder notifications

Third-Party Services

Qur’an Quotes does not use:

  • Analytics or tracking tools
  • Advertising networks
  • Cookies
  • External APIs or third-party services

Children’s Privacy

This extension does not knowingly collect any data from children or adults. Because no personal data is collected, it is safe for users of all ages.

Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated if features or permissions change. Any updates will be reflected on this page.
